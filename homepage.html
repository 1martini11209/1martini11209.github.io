TF2 Competitive Newbie Cup

Tournament to help get new players into competitive TF2. Join us in discord by clicking on the connect button below!

How to get started

The TF2 Competitive Newbie Cup is a one day event designed to help new players get better at the game and begin playing in competitive tournaments. Here's how to get started playing!

  1. Join our discord by using the connect button above.
  2. Join or create a team.
          Want to find a team?
         -In #bot_commands, say ?rank Looking For Team. This will give you the looking for team role, and captains can find you.
         -In #bot_commands, say ?rank CLASS. For example, ?rank Scout. Do this for each of your preferred classes.
         -If you want to take a more proactive approach, you can message captains with the Looking For Players role.

          Want to make a team?
         -In #bot_commands, say ?rank Looking For Players. This will give you the looking for players role.
         -Look on the member list on the left for members with the Looking For Team role. Message prospective members for your team.
         -Once you have your team together, message me or @Luigmas with a link to everybody on your team's steam profile.
          All of your team's members MUST have their hours in game on their profile set to public
